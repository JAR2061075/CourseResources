Social Media

Template Poetry Inline CSS

Social media is an online tool that allows you to interact with friends and family around the world. You can post comments on your profile about what you are up to. You can also post on other user's profile in some circumstances to respond to their comments or pictures. It also lets you get to know new people that may be friends of your contacts. In a short time, you could have quite a few "friends" online. Businesses also use this for marketing, often times giving special deals to those that "follow" them.

Social Media Comparison
